# Event Reminder Press Release and FAQ

## Press Release

Today we are announcing Event Reminder, a product designed to make missed events a rare exception. Event Reminder helps people and small teams schedule important events and receive timely, dependable reminders across their preferred channels.

Users create events once, choose reminder timing, and trust that alerts arrive at the right moment in their local timezone. Event Reminder focuses on speed, clarity, and reliability so users can stay focused on their work and life instead of constantly re-checking calendars.

With built-in delivery tracking, users can see whether reminders were sent, retried, or failed. This transparency builds confidence and supports real-world follow-through.

## FAQ

### Who is this for?
Busy professionals, families, and small teams that need dependable reminders for meetings, deadlines, and recurring tasks.

### What problem does this solve?
People forget important events because reminders are inconsistent, poorly timed, or hard to configure.

### What makes this different?
Reliability-first scheduling, timezone-safe behavior, and clear delivery status visibility.

### What is in scope for v1?
- Create and manage events
- Configure reminder offsets
- Basic multi-channel delivery abstraction
- Delivery status and retry visibility

### What is out of scope for v1?
- Full enterprise role management
- Deep third-party calendar sync
- Complex automation workflows
